About me

I am an assistant professor in School of Information Science at Japan Advance Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST) studying the field of Artificial Intelligence, Optimization, Modelling, Games Informatics, and Machine Learning.

Currently I am working closely with Hiroyuki Iida in Entertainment Technology at JAIST. Previously, I was a proud graduates of University of Science Malaysia, where I studied evolutionary computing under the supervision of Umi Kalsom Yusof for my B.S., M.Sc., and Ph.D. degree.

I study how can we integrate knowledge in real-world through games in order to improve quality of living.
